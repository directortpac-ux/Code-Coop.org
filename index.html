The Problem

Volunteer Departments Deserve Better Tools

Every year, volunteer first responders answer the call at 3 AM for their neighbors — on holidays, in storms, without hesitation. They deserve technology that works as hard as they do.

But commercial reporting systems cost $3,000 to $10,000+ per year — budgets that most volunteer departments simply don't have. The result? Paper-based reports, compliance gaps, missed certifications, and countless hours of volunteer time wasted on administrative busywork instead of training and response.

Code Co-Op exists to close that gap. We believe the departments that protect our communities should never be priced out of the technology they need to do it safely and effectively.

About Code Co-Op

Our Mission

"Built by Volunteer First Responders, for Volunteer First Responders"

Code Co-Op is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit building affordable, modern emergency reporting and department management tools for volunteer first responders. We believe the departments that protect our communities should not be priced out of the technology they need to do it safely and effectively.

Code Co-Op is built by volunteer first responders who understand the challenges of running a volunteer department — because we live them every day. Our cooperative model keeps costs low, features relevant, and development driven by the people who actually use the tools in the field.

Community-Driven

Development priorities are set by the volunteer departments who use the platform. Your feedback directly shapes the product.

Cooperative Model

Our cooperative structure keeps costs low and ensures the platform serves its users — not shareholders or investors.

Built in the Field

Our developers are actual first responders. Every feature is designed from real experience on the fireground and in the ambulance.
